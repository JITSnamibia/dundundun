Google Search Console Setup

To get indexed, your site must be accessible and verified in Google Search Console. Here's how to ensure this is done right:

1

Add your domain to Google Search Console

Use the 'Domain' property option for full domain coverage.

2

Get TXT record from Google

Google will give you a TXT record to add to your domain's DNS settings on GoDaddy.

3

Wait and verify

After adding the TXT record, wait for DNS propagation, then verify in Search Console.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important

If Google Search Console cannot verify your domain, it will not index your site. Use a tool like DNS Checker to confirm the TXT record is visible globally.
